ABOUT

COSMITTO, which derives its etymology from the latin origin and translet to commit or to entrust is among the top five festivals of the eastern india and the biggest of its kind in Odisha.
Though it’s quiet young as compared to its most established festivals, COSMITTO witnesses a footfall over 700 people, studentts coming from more than 10 colleges to participate in over 40 events, with its seamless blend of competitive bout and incessant exuberance, COSMITTO provides the perfect platform to budding engineers to show their mettle.
